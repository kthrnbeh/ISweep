Frequently Asked Questions

What does ISweep actually do?

ISweep listens to your audio and observes your video in real time. When it detects content you’ve chosen to filter, it can mute, skip, or fast-forward automatically—without changing the original media.

Which devices does ISweep work with?

You can link ISweep to supported Smart TVs, streaming sticks, and companion devices. Check the Devices section in your Account page to see which devices you’ve connected.

Will ISweep work with all streaming services?

ISweep is designed to work at the system level, so it can observe and filter content from most streaming apps, DVDs, and connected devices, as long as your device is supported.

Can I customize what is filtered for each child?

Yes. You can create different profiles with their own filter settings under Settings > Parental Controls, so each child gets an experience that matches your family rules.

Troubleshooting

  • No sound? Check that your TV and streaming device audio are not both muted and that ISweep is not set to “Mute Only” for all events.
  • Filters feel too strict? Lower the sensitivity slider or switch some categories to “Log only” in Settings.
  • ISweep not responding? Make sure your device is online and that ISweep is still linked under Devices.
