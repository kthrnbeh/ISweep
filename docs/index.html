How it works

  1. Install

    Add ISweep to your Smart TV (or companion device). Pair with your streaming source or DVD player.

  2. Choose Filters

    Remove profanity, sexuality, and violence. Set actions: mute, skip, or fast-forward.

  3. Enable Observer Mode

    ISweep listens and detects events in real time—then applies your rules automatically.

  4. Review & Refine

    Check your session log and adjust sensitivity and actions for next time.
